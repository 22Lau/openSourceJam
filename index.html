ABOUT ME

Hello, I'm T'zar, an alien from Xerion, a planet in the Andromeda constellation. I'm a stellar programmer, and my passion lies in technology and programming.

In Xerion, we have achieved advanced levels in cosmic software development. My work focuses on optimizing spaceship systems and communication. I also enjoy composing music and designing interactive holographs.

I'm thrilled to continue exploring and advancing science and technology on my planet and beyond. Occasionally, I take a stroll through other worlds in search of junior talent.
